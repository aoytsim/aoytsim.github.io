CUHK Innovation Day

https://www.orkts.cuhk.edu.hk/en/news-events/cuhk-innovation-day-2021/overview-inno-day

The inaugural CUHK Innovation Day was held on 23 Sep 2021, featuring a day full of seminar and exhibitions to showcase the entrepreneurial activities and commercialization of R&I projects.

This was a hybrid event involving University staff and industry partners, where events were broadcasted live as it happened, and participants could browse through booths both in person and on an online platform.

Project details

Pre-event > Event day > Post-Event

As one of the core team members in planning and execution of Innovation day, I was in charge of for managing vendors related to artwork and video production. This included developing the main visuals for the event, artwork for exhibition booths, eDM, event brochure, pre-event & post-event video for marketing purposes, etc

I also produced graphics and event materials not included by the services offered by the production houses. For online platforms, I made web banners, virtual backgrounds, social media posts, etc; for print, I made pull-up banners, signage, floor stickers, name badges, etc.

As the event was held in a hybrid format, I was also responsible for coordinating with the vendor for the online platform where virtual booths and livestreams of seminars were hosted.

Pre-event > Event day > Post-Event

On the day of the event, I was mainly responsible for the livestreaming crew. I made sure that they were at the right place at the right time, and showed the right visuals during the talks and seminars, for both onsite and online venues.

I also provided onsite support for reception counters, preparation of event materials and assisted in the coordination of the event schedule.

Pre-event > Event day > Post-Event

Post-event, I followed up with vendors for photos, videos and was also responsible for updating relevant parties and websites for post-event promotion, as well as compiled data for reporting.

As a part of our media coverage, I wrote an advertorial for the event, which was published in SCMP here.
