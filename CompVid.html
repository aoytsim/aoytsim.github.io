Project details

Produced company videos for multiple student startup companies, with the final deliverable being an introduction video for their use.

For this I had to coordinate with multiple parties to facilitate the shoot, including the student startup themselves, administration for venue booking, and multiple meetings with the video crew and producers to decide the direction and storyline of the videos.

I was also present on the day of the shoot, to ensure the quallity of the interview footage taken and suggest any impromptu scenes.

Afterwards, I closely followed up with the companies and video editor to ensure the quality and accuracy of the final cut, including the audio, on-screen graphic and visuals, as well as the subtitles (Chinese & English).
