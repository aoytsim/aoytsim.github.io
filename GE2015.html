Global Elites 2015

​http://roundtablecommunity.org.hk/ge/

A programme aimed to nurture 'global leaders', organised and ran by university students, for secondary school students in Hong Kong. Includes a 3-day 2-night camp, mock UN conference, and a series of training workshops.

Project details

Worked as the sole designer for the entire project, and as a member of the organising committee.

I was in charge of coming up with the main visual, liaising with multiple parties on designs of various items required for the programme. This includes: promotional material such as posters and social media graphics; as well as event material such as table stands, t-shirts, name tags, and study guides.

In a team of six, I also participated actively in the planning and execution of one of the main events of the youth camp. Participants are put into a role-playing game, where they need to use their deductive skills to 'uncover' certain clues and truths.

The theme of the programme was: Media (傳媒解碼 破迷尋真)
